ExpColumns =

Pattern for parsing experience columns

Examples:

Matches: “Sword: 100 34% Dabbling”

/(?:\s*(?<skill>[a-zA-Z\s]+)\b:\s*(?<rank>\d+)\s+(?<percent>\d+)%\s+(?<rate>[a-zA-Z\s]+)\b)/.freeze
BriefExpOn =

Pattern for parsing brief experience output when enabled

Examples:

Matches: “<component id=‘exp Sword’>Sword: 100 34% [12/34]</component>”

%r{<component id='exp .*?<d cmd='skill (?<skill>[a-zA-Z\s]+)'.*:\s+(?<rank>\d+)\s+(?<percent>\d+)%\s*\[\s?(?<rate>\d+)\/34\].*?<\/component>}.freeze
BriefExpOff =

Pattern for parsing brief experience output when disabled
